Bootcamp Project - Bakery: Docelene

Web design (UX/UI) and logo design (branding).

About this project

Goal

This fictional bakery is a high end bakery, specialized in muffins, which has a goal of being known as the most elegant and prestige bakery in Porto.
It needs to have a design that reflects its values and that shows that.

Problem

Bakery had no online presence and no way to claim the prestige it was desired to have.
It needed a way to make sure people understood right away, without reading, without thinking much, that the product would be delicious, an experience, a moment, expensive but worth it.

Solution

I designed a website and logo that match with eachother, with the same colors and typography.
The website has a very strong identity and very a different style from what a normal bakery normally has.
Website was designed based on the values of the bakery and in a way to attract the ideal customer.
Nothing was randomly chosen for the website design or logo design.

My duties and tools

- Wireframes and design style guide, in Adobe XD
- Logo, in Adobe Photoshop
- Moodboard, in Adobe Illustrator

My influence

I decided that the web design and logo design, had to have a very defined style so that even without a logo or many words, people would recognize the bakery in any platform (website, social media, newsletters, google ads).
I created the name and the logo aswell, which was very thoughtful and detailed, and I'll be glad to explain it to you in the near future.

Difficulties/What I learned

Since it was a bootcamp project of a fictional bakery, there are no real users, no user research, so I tried to imagine what the pain, needs, motivations and fears of an ideal or possible customer and how they would relate to the product. I then designed based on that information for their best possible experience using the product.
