My design process

01 Discovery

Identify the core challenges that stand in the way of business growth and success.

02 Strategy

Define the goals.
Design towards the best design to solve the problems.

03 Wireframing

Design the low-fidelity wireframes to make sure structure and the core functionality is going as it should.

04 Visual Design

Design the visual for the digital product in the form of high-fidelity wireframes.

05 Test

Make sure everything is working as it should, small details can be hard to catch.

06 Delivery

Provide all of the work and leave suggestions.
