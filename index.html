My design process

01 Discovery

My first step is to identify the core challenges that stand in the way of business growth and success. Understanding what is needed, what is in the scope to then find the best possible solution for the problems.

02 Strategy

Define the goals.
Research the personas, competitors, and best practices.
Create the structure of the information.
Design towards the best design to solve the problems.
Present the project.

03 Wireframing

Design the low-fidelity wireframes to make sure structure and the core functionality is going as it should.

04 Visual Design

Designing the visual for the digital product in the form of high-fidelity wireframes.

05 Test

I make sure everything is working as it should, small details can be hard to catch.

06 Delivery

At the end of the project, I provide all of the work I did and give my thoughts on it.
